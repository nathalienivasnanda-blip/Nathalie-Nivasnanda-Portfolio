About Nathalie Nivasnanda

Hello, welcome to my writing portfolio! My name is Nathalie (pronounced like "Natalie"). I'm a Technical Writer and Documentation Quality Analyst based in the US. Getting my start as a Technical Writer in December 2020, I've worked with a number of different clients primarily in the technology world doing all things documents related. From working on user guides, manuals, standard operating procedures, process diagrams, reports, fliers, and more, you'll likely find me behind a computer screen writing or playing around with a new tool!

I have an educational background in Writing, Rhetoric and Discourse where I obtained my Bachelors and Masters of Arts in Writing, Rhetoric and Discourse from DePaul University.

On this portfolio, you will find some writing samples and my resume. If you would like to connect, feel free to get in touch with me at nathalie.nivasnanda@gmail.com!
